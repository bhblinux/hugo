相关配置
1. emacs配置
#+BEGIN_SRC lisp
(setq server-auth-dir "")
(setq server-name "eamcs-server-file")
(server-start)
#+END_SRC
2. windows配置
设置环境变量
#+BEGIN_SRC 
EMACS_SERVER_FILE 与 ALTERNATE_EDITOR环境变量
EMACS_SERVER_FILE变量,值为"d:\emacs-server-file"
ALTERNATE_EDITOR=d:\emacs-23.3\runemacs.exe

#+END_SRC

#+BEGIN_SRC
